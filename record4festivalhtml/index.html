INDIAN FESTIVALS


DIWALI

Diwali (also called Deepavali) is one of the biggest and most widely celebrated festivals in India. It’s known as the “Festival of Lights”, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance.


ONAM

Onam is the biggest festival of Kerala, celebrated with great joy and unity by Malayalis across the world. Onam reflects values of prosperity, equality, and togetherness. It is both a harvest festival and a cultural celebration symbolizing Kerala’s rich traditions.


HOLI

Holi is one of the most joyful and colorful festivals of India, popularly known as the “Festival of Colors”.
